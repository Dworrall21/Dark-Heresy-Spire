# The Saint with the Wrong Shadow v0.1

A first playtest mission for **Dark Heresy Spire**.

Designed to test:

- Investigation
- Heat/Subtlety
- Requisition
- Patron pressure
- Psychic or occult threat
- Forbidden shortcuts
- Quiet route
- Loud route
- Disaster route

This mission is built using the project’s **Campaign -> Mission -> Operation -> Situation** structure and the "never hide the core clue" rule from the Mission/Operation template.

---

# 1. Mission Premise

A minor shrine in Hive Olymra has reported a miracle.

A dead hab-worker named **Sella Vorn** has been declared locally holy after her corpse failed to decay, her wounds bled clean water, and pilgrims claimed her shadow moved independently beneath the shrine-lamps.

The Ecclesiarchy wants the miracle authenticated.

The local governor wants the crowds controlled.

The Adeptus Arbites want the shrine shut down.

The Patron suspects something worse: Sella’s "wrong shadow" may be a psychic imprint, daemonic parasite, xenos residue, or deliberate hoax covering a larger heresy.

The cell is sent quietly.

They are not to reveal the Inquisition unless absolutely necessary.

```text

Forbidden Temptation: Use daemonological consultation, memory work, or a controlled purge to solve the problem quickly.
Mission Failure State: The wrong shadow manifests during mass devotion and marks hundreds of pilgrims.
```

# 3. What Is Really Happening

Sella Vorn was not a saint.

She was a tired manufactorum hab-worker who unknowingly carried a fragment of a forbidden relic: a small black glass bead called the **Noctilith Tear**.

The Tear was smuggled into Hive Olymra by a small cult called **The Choir of the Last Candle**. The cult believes suffering, witnessed by enough faithful eyes, can "teach the soul to cast a better shadow."

Sella stole the bead from her overseer, thinking it was valuable enough to buy passage for her daughter out of the district. When cult enforcers cornered her, she hid inside Saint Septima’s chapel and died during a panic crush.

Now the relic is lodged inside her corpse.

The "miracle" is not preserving her.

It is using public devotion as fuel.

The wrong shadow is an embryonic warp-echo learning how to imitate sainthood.

# 10. Core Clue Web

## Core Revelation

The miracle is caused by a forbidden relic hidden inside Sella Vorn’s corpse. The relic feeds on devotion and is being cultivated by the Choir of the Last Candle.

## Core Clue

Sella’s shadow moves toward prayer, not light.

This can be discovered by observation, witness testimony, auspex scan, Witch-Sight, forensic examination, or reviewing chapel picts.

Do not hide this clue. A failed roll determines what it costs to learn.

## Clues

| Clue | Type | What It Reveals | Possible Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The shadow bends toward prayer.** | Physical / Occult | The effect responds to devotion, not light. | Mind or Corruption stress. |
| **Sella’s death record was altered after her death.** | Documentary | Someone wanted the body moved to the chapel ledger. | Shadow or Authority stress. |
| **Her daughter Mira vanished the same night.** | Social | Sella was trying to escape someone. | Human cost; possible Bond pressure. |
| **The corpse wound contains black glass residue.** | Physical / Forbidden | A relic or shard is inside the body. | Corruption stress or Ecclesiarchy conflict. |
| **Pilgrims healed by the miracle share the same dream.** | Occult / Social | The wrong shadow is networking through devotion. | Warp Trace clock. |
| **A candle seller uses coded phrases near the chapel line.** | Social | Cult recruitment is active among pilgrims. | Enemy Awareness +1 if mishandled. |
| **Pict feeds show a priest alone with the corpse.** | Technical | Chapel leadership is hiding something, but not necessarily guilty. | False lead. |
| **A servitor repeats Sella’s last words without being programmed.** | Technical / Occult | The relic has contaminated local machines. | Mechanicus interest. |
| **Sella’s overseer owned the Noctilith Tear first.** | Documentary / Social | The cult is embedded in the manufactorum supply chain. | Cult Escape Route advances. |
| **The wrong shadow has no reflection in polished silver.** | Occult | Warded or sanctified methods can reveal it. | Heat if done publicly. |

## False Lead

The chapel’s junior priest, **Frater Loam**, has been faking minor healings to encourage donations. He is corrupt and frightened, but he did not create the miracle.

## Dangerous Truth

Some pilgrims have genuinely been healed.

The healing is real.

The source is not holy.

# 13. Mission Routes

## Quiet Completion Route

The cell:

1. Maintains audit cover.
2. Gains private access to Sella’s body.
3. Finds Mira and protects her.
4. Identifies Cale and the Choir.
5. Requisitions warded containment.
6. Removes the Noctilith Tear during a controlled mortuary transfer.
7. Lets the Ecclesiarchy announce that Sella’s body has been moved for authentication.

**Success:** Mission complete, Heat stays below 6, Patron satisfied.

**Cost:** The public may still believe Sella is a saint.

## Loud Completion Route

The cell:

1. Invokes authority.
2. Locks down the chapel.
3. Raids the cult.
4. Publicly suppresses the vigil.
5. Extracts or destroys the relic under armed guard.

**Success:** Threat contained.

**Cost:** Heat 6+, Ecclesiarchy Complaint likely fills, Patron Notice likely marked.

## Disaster Route

Any of the following can trigger disaster:

- The wrong shadow awakens.
- Mira becomes a prayer-vector.
- The cell kills pilgrims publicly.
- The cell reveals daemonic truth to the crowd.
- The cult escapes with the second relic fragment.
- The cell bargains with the shadow for a true answer.

**Failure State:** Heat 9, Public Panic fills, and the Patron demands cleanup.

# 15. Forbidden Shortcuts

These should work, but create sharp costs.

## Daemonological Consultation

Ask one true question about the wrong shadow, the Noctilith Tear, or the rite.

**Works because:** The entity wants to be understood.

**Cost:** D3 or D6 Corruption stress, Warp Trace, Patron Notice if discovered.

**Complication:** The answer includes a prayer the shadow wants spoken aloud.

## Memory Work on Mira

Recover Sella’s final moments from Mira’s damaged memory.

**Works because:** Mira saw enough to identify Cale and the bead.

**Cost:** D3 Mind and D3 Corruption stress; Humanity Beat pressure.

**Complication:** Mira remembers the cell’s face as part of the trauma.

## Excruciator Protocol on Cale

Get the cult structure and rite schedule immediately.

**Works because:** Cale knows enough and is afraid of pain.

**Cost:** D3 Mind or Authority; D6 Shadow if exposed.

**Complication:** Cale gives one true answer and one answer he thinks will make the pain stop.

## Controlled Purge

Burn the chapel, corpse, cult, and pilgrims before the wrong shadow awakens.

**Works because:** Fire and mass death end the immediate vector.

**Cost:** Heat +2 minimum, D6 Authority, possible Severe Mind or Corruption Fallout.

**Complication:** Something survives inside the ashes.

## Bargain with the Wrong Shadow

Ask the shadow who brought the relic to Olymra.

**Works because:** It knows.

**Cost:** D8 Corruption, Patron Notice, Warp Claim clock.

**Complication:** It asks to be named a saint in return.

# 18. Rewards and Consequences

## Clean Success

The cell contains the Noctilith Tear, identifies the Choir, protects Mira, and keeps Heat below 6.

Reward options:

- D8 Patron Requisition.
- Warded containment case as D6 Resource.
- Mira as a D6 Contact.
- Ecclesiarchy access as D6 Resource.
- One clue toward the second relic fragment.

## Partial Success

The threat is contained, but with public scandal, lost evidence, escaped cultists, or institutional anger.

Consequences:

- Heat converts into a campaign clock.
- Ecclesiarchy or Arbites becomes Suspicious.
- Cale escapes or dies before naming the wider network.
- The Patron asks why the cell missed the relic’s true name.

## Failure

The wrong shadow awakens or the relic escapes.

Consequences:

- The district gains a false saint cult.
- Mira becomes marked.
- Patron Notice immediately fills to 3.
- Rival Inquisition interest begins.
- Next mission starts at Heat 3+.
