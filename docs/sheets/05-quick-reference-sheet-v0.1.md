# Quick Reference Sheet v0.1

This is a table-facing rules reference for play.

---

# Core Roll

Build a pool of D10s.

Start with **1D10**.

Add +1D10 for each:

| Source | Add? |
|---|---:|
| Relevant Skill | ☐ |
| Relevant Domain | ☐ |
| Mastery | ☐ |
| Help | ☐ |
| Gear / Resource / Advantage | ☐ |

Roll the full pool.

Then apply Difficulty.

---

# Difficulty

| Difficulty | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Safe** | Roll normally. |
| **Risky** | Remove the highest die after rolling. |
| **Dangerous** | Remove the two highest dice after rolling. |
| **Impossible** | Roll as Dangerous, and stress/fallout/exposure/escalation remains possible even on success. |

If all dice are removed, treat the final result as **1**.

---

# Results

| Highest Remaining Die | Result |
|---|---|
| **1** | Critical failure. Fail and take double stress. |
| **2-5** | Failure. Do not get what you wanted and take stress. |
| **6-7** | Success at cost. Get what you wanted, but take stress. |
| **8-9** | Clean success. |
| **10** | Critical success. Extra benefit. |

For attacks or direct harm, a 10 also adds **+1 stress inflicted** for each 10 rolled.

---

# Stress Dice

| Threat Level | Stress Die | Example |
|---|---:|---|
| **Incidental** | D3 | Minor suspicion, bruises, embarrassment |
| **Risky** | D6 | Armed cultists, hard interrogation, dangerous stealth |
| **Dangerous** | D8 | Heavy weapons, psychic threat, noble exposure |
| **Catastrophic** | D10 | Daemonhost, orbital politics, public scandal |

---

# Resistances

| Resistance | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **Body** | Injury, exhaustion, poison, radiation, disease, deprivation |
| **Mind** | Fear, trauma, guilt, doubt, obsession, memory damage |
| **Shadow** | Cover, secrecy, subtlety, aliases, surveillance, evidence |
| **Authority** | Patron trust, faction standing, legality, chain of command |
| **Corruption** | Warp-taint, forbidden knowledge, xenos influence, mutation, possession |

---

# Fallout Check

Each time you take stress:

1. Add the stress result to the relevant Resistance.
2. Roll 1D10.
3. If the result is **lower than your current stress** in that Resistance, you suffer Fallout.

| Severity | Stress Range | Meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| **Minor** | 1-4 | Immediate trouble, short-term harm, fixable problem |
| **Moderate** | 5-8 | Serious injury, lasting pressure, dangerous scrutiny |
| **Severe** | 9+ | Permanent harm, destroyed cover, major corruption, mission-changing consequence |
| **Critical** | Special | Character-ending or campaign-altering consequence |

---

# Clearing Stress After Fallout

| Fallout Severity | Stress Cleared |
|---|---|
| **Minor** | Clear all stress in the triggering Resistance. |
| **Moderate** | Clear all stress in the triggering Resistance and D3 stress from one other Resistance. |
| **Severe** | Clear all stress in the triggering Resistance and all stress from one other Resistance. |
| **Critical** | Clear stress only if the character survives in playable form. |

---

# Heat

| Heat | State | Meaning |
|---:|---|---|
| **0-2** | **Ghosts** | Hidden. The cell chooses the moment. |
| **3-5** | **Rumors** | Someone noticed. Cover is being tested. |
| **6-8** | **Hunted** | Enemies, authorities, or rivals are moving. |
| **9+** | **Burned** | Quiet completion is no longer possible without major sacrifice. |

Heat rises when the cell leaves:

- Witnesses
- Records
- Bodies
- Psychic traces
- Public authority
- Forbidden evidence
- Obvious violence
- Compromised cover
- Factional complaints

---

# Enemy Awareness

| Awareness | What They Know |
|---:|---|
| **0** | Nothing. The cell is invisible. |
| **1** | Something is wrong. |
| **2** | Someone is investigating. |
| **3** | A covert cell is active. |
| **4** | The Inquisition may be involved. |
| **5** | They know the cell's methods. |
| **6** | They know names, faces, aliases, gene-signs, or soul-impressions. |
| **7** | They strike first. |

---

# Cover Damage

| Cover Damage | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Scuffed** | Step Cover down one die size, or take D3 Shadow stress. |
| **Questioned** | The next roll relying on Cover is Risky. |
| **Flagged** | Cover works, but its use raises Heat by 1 until repaired. |
| **Compromised** | Cover only works in limited places or with additional proof. |
| **Exposed** | Burn Cover or increase Heat by 1. |
| **Collapsed** | Burn Cover and increase Heat by 1. If Patron-backed, mark Patron Notice. |

---

# Flashing the Rosette

When an acolyte flashes the Rosette or equivalent authority, choose one benefit:

- Gain Mastery on Command, Procure, or Investigate.
- Turn a failed Requisition into success at cost.
- Force an Imperial official to comply.
- Bypass ordinary legal restrictions.
- Command access to a location, prisoner, archive, or restricted object.
- Seize jurisdiction from a local faction.
- Freeze a scene long enough for the cell to act.

Then choose one cost:

- Heat +1 if private or contained.
- Heat +2 if public, recorded, or politically explosive.
- D6 Authority stress.
- D6 Shadow stress.
- Mark Patron Notice.
- Trigger a Patron Liability.
- Create a factional enemy.

---

# Patron Notice

Mark Patron Notice when:

- A Major or Severe Beat is completed.
- The cell publicly invokes Inquisitorial authority.
- A Patron Boon is used loudly or dramatically.
- Heat reaches 3, 6, or 9 for the first time in an Operation.
- The cell suffers Severe Authority, Shadow, or Corruption fallout.
- The cell delays or contradicts the Mandate.
- A Red Line is approached.

At **3 Patron Notice**, the Patron makes a Move, then clear Patron Notice.

---

# Beats

At the start of each session, each player chooses:

| Beat Type | Theme |
|---|---|
| **Duty** | Obedience, purity, mission, command, sacrifice, official violence |
| **Humanity** | Mercy, loyalty, doubt, civilian life, confession, restraint |
| **Radical** | Forbidden tools, expedience, secrecy, corruption, leverage, survival |

| Beat Completed | Reward |
|---|---|
| **Minor Beat** | Minor Advance or D6 Requisition |
| **Major Beat** | Major Advance or D8 Requisition |
| **Severe Beat** | Severe Advance, D10 Requisition, or permanent Patron Boon |

# End-of-Situation Checklist

At the end of each Situation, ask:

1. Did anyone see what happened?
2. Did anyone record what happened?
3. Did anyone survive with useful information?
4. Did the cell use loud violence, psychic power, forbidden tools, or Inquisitorial authority?
5. Did a Cover Identity explain the event?
6. Did the enemy learn something specific?
7. Did the Patron have reason to notice?
8. Did any faction gain a reason to intervene?
9. Did any evidence remain unsecured?
10. Did the cell cross or approach a Red Line?

Then:

1. Adjust Heat.
2. Adjust Enemy Awareness if needed.
3. Apply Shadow stress or Fallout if needed.
4. Trigger threshold moves if Heat reached 3, 6, or 9.
5. Mark Patron Notice if appropriate.
6. Tick any Operation clocks.
7. Announce what the players can perceive changing.
