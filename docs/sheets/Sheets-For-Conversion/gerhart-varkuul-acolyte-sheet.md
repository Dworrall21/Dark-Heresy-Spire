## Translation Notes

**Character Summary:**

Gerhart is a **melee-focused Secutor** translated from a high-power Genesys Dark Heresy character (DH 3.3.1).

**Key Mapping Decisions:**

- **Brawn 6 + Melee 6 → Fight Mastery (melee only).** Per design intent, he only gets Mastery in melee combat, not ranged. His Agility 2 means he's a terrible shot.
- **Soak 7 → Protection 3.** Heavy Power Armor + Subskin + Toughened. "Heavy, rare, powered, or story-level" protection.
- **Wounds 21, Strain 17 → Resistance tracks.** No separate wound/strain numbers in Spire. His high physical resilience is expressed through Body Protection 3 and multiple defensive advances.
- **Parry/Block/Bulwark/Defensive → Bodyguard Reflex (Major Advance).** All defensive talents collapse into one ability: take Body fallout for allies.
- **Augmentations → Severe Advance with Burden.** Powerful but exposure risk.
- **Talents → Advances.** 20+ Genesys talents collapse into 3 Minor + 3 Major + 2 Severe advances.
- **Cover → Knight-Protector of the Faith.** Based on his feudal world origin, his mentor's armor, and his zealous personality.
- **Equipment → Resources.** Weapons and armor use Spire's Resource die system.

**What makes him powerful:**
- Fight Mastery (melee) + Protection 3 = nearly unkillable in melee
- Bodyguard Reflex = protects the whole cell
- Three high-quality weapons with special tags
- D10 Bond to faith = massive resolve
- Patron backing with D8 resource die

**What keeps him balanced:**
- No ranged Mastery (Agility 2)
- Zero social skills
- Zero subtlety (Shadow Protection 0)
- Corruption stress already at 1 from augmentations
- Garlic pendant superstition = GM narrative hook
- Duty beat could conflict with cell tactics
- Augmentations are a ticking Burden
