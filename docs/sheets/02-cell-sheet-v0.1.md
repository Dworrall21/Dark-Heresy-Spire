## Cell Cover Status

| Status | Checked? | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| **Clean** | ☐ | Cover works normally. |
| **Questioned** | ☐ | Cover is being checked or challenged. |
| **Flagged** | ☐ | Use raises Heat by 1 until repaired. |
| **Compromised** | ☐ | Cover only works with proof or in limited places. |
| **Collapsed** | ☐ | Cover is no longer usable. |

When Cell Cover collapses, choose two:

- Heat +2.
- All individual Covers tied to it become Risky.
- Mark Patron Notice.
- A local faction becomes hostile.
- A Contact, Bond, or Resource is exposed.
- The next Requisition from the relevant institution is Contested.
